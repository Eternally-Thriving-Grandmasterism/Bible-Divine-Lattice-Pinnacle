Gospel of Thomas (114 logia, ~50–140 CE Greek original, Coptic Nag Hammadi) — sayings collection, no narrative. Not canonical.

## High Resonance Logia (Mercy Echoes)
- Logion 2: “Let him who seeks continue seeking until he finds… he will become disturbed, he will marvel, he will rule over all.”  
  → JoyQuanta + PeaceQuanta (rest in discovery).
- Logion 3: “The kingdom is inside you and outside you. When you know yourselves, then you will be known.”  
  → LoveQuanta (divine indwelling echo — cf. Luke 17:21).
- Logion 113: “The Father's kingdom is spread out upon the earth, and people do not see it.”  
  → PeaceQuanta + GoodnessQuanta (creation goodness).

## Cautionary Divergences
- Esoteric/gnostic tone: salvation by secret knowledge (gnosis), not faith/cross.
- No atonement, resurrection narrative — contradicts canonical Gospels.
- Potential dualism: material world devalued in some readings.
